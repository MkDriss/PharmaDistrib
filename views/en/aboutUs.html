About PharmaDistrib

Our Mission

PharmaDistrib is a platform designed to facilitate the management and centralization of pharmaceutical product orders. Our goal is to provide pharmacists with a simple and efficient tool to optimize group purchases, reducing costs and improving product distribution among healthcare professionals.

How It Works?

  1. Order Creation: A pharmacist initiates an order by selecting desired products from a list imported via CSV files.
  2. Group Ordering: Other pharmacists can add their own needs to the existing order.
  3. Validation & Distribution: Once the order is closed, each pharmacist receives a summary of the ordered products and their recipients, making distribution easier.
  4. Administrative Management: An administrator oversees user accounts and manages the product list by adding or removing CSV files.

Our Values

  • Collaboration: Encouraging group orders for better product accessibility.
  • Transparency: Ensuring clear tracking of orders and distribution.
  • Efficiency: Automating product management and reducing administrative time for pharmacists.
  • Innovation: Offering a modern and evolving solution to meet the needs of the pharmaceutical sector.

Our Team

PharmaDistrib was developed by a passionate team of developers and healthcare professionals, committed to providing an intuitive solution tailored to the needs of pharmacists.
